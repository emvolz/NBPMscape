This folder contains files used to compute the regional probabilities of importation given an importation into the UK.

File: importation_prob.R
Description: Compute importation probability by region based on passenger numbers using Civil Aviation Authority data

File: CAA_2024_uk_airport.csv
Description: List of airports and postcodes (manually collated)

File: CAA_pax_2024_ITL2.rds
Description: Output from importation_prob.R containing ITL2 regions in England and Wales and the probability of air arrivals based on airport passenger arrivals in 2024
